Campus Life

school cafeteria

The SAI International School cafeteria features a spacious, large and hygienic eating place. The seating has been immaculately designed to keep the cafeteria hassle free during rush hours.

school library

To enrich our students, we at SAI International have four well-stocked libraries in 3 different wings of the school, catering to the needs and likes of both junior and senior students. From daily newspapers, periodicals, magazines, course related books, autobiographies, encyclopedias, manuscripts to story books, novels, bestsellers, classics etc, the libraries are an organized collection of documented information from across the globe.

school auditorium

The Auditoriums of a school are the hub of activities. SAI International School has two magnificent state-of-the-art auditoriums, well equipped modern stage lighting, digital sound systems with ample space to benefit the students. These multipurpose auditoriums are used for meetings, assemblies, seminars, celebrations, drama, choirs, orchestras, and other extra-curricular activities.

Won D.L.Shah Quality Award for two times in a row

SAI International School is the only School in the Country to bag the coveted D.L.Shah Quality Award for two times in a row for achieving excellence through quality initiatives. The Quality Council of India (QCI) instituted the QCI-D.L.Shah Award to promote quality excellence for the wellbeing of the citizens of the country. The Award is given annually to recognize successful organization in all spheres of activity and promote awareness on adopting excellent standard. Dr. Bijaya Kumar Sahoo, Founder & Chief Mentor, SAI International Education Group received the D.L.Shah Quality Award 2017 for the first time at its National Quality Conclave, held at New Delhi on Friday, September 22, 2017. QCI once again conferred SAI International School with the prestigious D.L.Shah Award 2018. The Award was presented to Founder, Dr. Sahoo, at the 12th Quality Conclave, held at Pune on Tuesday, February 12, 2019.

Rank 1 School of India in Day-cum-Boarding School category, as per EducationWorld India School Rankings (EWISR), 2020

SAI International School created history by becoming the Rank 1 School of India in Day-cum-Boarding School category, as per EducationWorld India School Rankings (EWISR), 2020. This is for the first time a school from Eastern India bagged the position of India’s No.1 School in its 73 years of Independence. SAI International School elevated its position from being the No 3 School in India in 2019 to the No 1 School in India this year, along with DPS R.K.Puram, Delhi, leaving behind eminent schools like Greenwood High, Bangalore, The Emerald Heights International School, Indore, The Hyderabad Public School, Hyderabad, Modern School, Barakhamba Road, Delhi, Pallikoodam School, Kottayam, The Sanskaar Valley School, Bhopal, DPS, Vasant Kunj, Delhi, DPS, Noida, to win the top position. This makes SAI the only School in India to become a top rated school of the country in the category in less than 12 years of establishment, making the achievement indeed historic.

Business World bestowed the coveted ‘School of the Year Award’

Business World bestowed the coveted ‘School of the Year Award’, under the K12 School category on SAI International School during an online ceremony held on Saturday, November 28, 2020. This recognition earns the School an elite place in the Business World Top Education Brands Club. Adding to its joy, SAI International Residential School received the Leading Residential Schools in India Award 2020 under the category of Residential School.
